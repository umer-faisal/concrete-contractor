Expert Resurfacing Overview

Concrete resurfacing is a cost-effective solution for renewing worn, cracked, or stained concrete surfaces. This process involves applying a thin layer of polymer-modified concrete over the existing surface, resulting in a smooth, durable finish that enhances both the appearance and functionality of your concrete.

Whether it's your driveway, patio, or commercial space, concrete resurfacing can significantly improve the overall aesthetic and structural integrity of your surfaces, making it an ideal choice for both residential and commercial properties.

How long does concrete resurfacing last?

With proper care, resurfaced concrete can last up to 10 years or more, depending on usage and maintenance.

Can I walk on resurfaced concrete immediately?

Typically, you can walk on it within 24 hours, but we recommend waiting 48 hours before heavy use.

Is concrete resurfacing suitable for all types of concrete?

Yes, resurfacing can be applied to most concrete surfaces, including driveways, patios, and commercial floors.
